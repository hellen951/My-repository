Vegan Vodka Pasta

This vegan vodka pasta is my all time favorite pasta dish. Perfectly cooked rigatoni tossed in a savory and creamy vegan vodka sauce. Yum!

Course Main dish
Cuisine Italian
Diet Vegan, Vegetarian
Prep Time 10 minutes
Cook Time 20 minutes
Total Time 30 minutes
Servings 4 servings
Calories 386kcal

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pasta (rigatoni)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ red onion, small diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 6 oz tomato paste (canned)
  • ¼ cup vodka
  • 14.5 oz coconut milk (canned)
  • 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast
  • ½ cup reserved pasta water
  • Fresh chopped parsley (optional garnish)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, add the rigatoni and cook until al dente; drain but reserve ½ cup of the cooking water for use in the sauce.
  2. While the pasta cooks, get started on the sauce by using a large skillet or pot over medium heat, then adding olive oil, red onion, garlic, salt, and peppers and cooking, stirring frequently, until the onions begin to soften.
  3. Add the tomato paste and cook an additional 2-3 minutes.
  4. Pour in the vodka and allow the alcohol to evaporate, about 4-5 minutes.
  5. Stir in the coconut milk and nutritional yeast, and bring to a light simmer, then slowly add the pasta water until desired consistency has been reached.
  6. Serve the sauce over the noodles and garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
